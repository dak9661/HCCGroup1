Objective

The objective of this app is to allow its users to sign in and allow it to scan class syllubi to auto populate important dates and assignments onto a calender of their choosing. Also allows for simple psuh notifictaions that can remind users of important dates. The main goal of the app is to allow for fast and simple organization.
